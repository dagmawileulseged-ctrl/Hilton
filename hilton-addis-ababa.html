An urban oasis near
Addis Ababa's bustling center

Set within lush gardens, our urban retreat is near ECA Convention Center, Unity Park, Science & Technology Museum, and Friendship Park. We can arrange tours to various attractions nearby.

We offer a shuttle service to Bole International Airport, just a 20-minute ride away. Enjoy our geothermal pool, fitness center, and tennis courts.
